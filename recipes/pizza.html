Pizza

Description

This is a simple pepperoni pizza that you can easily make at home.

Who doesn't love pizza? :)

Ingredients

  • Dough ball or pre-made pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Spice/seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450C
  2. Add sauce to dough
  3. Add cheese on top of sauce
  4. Add pepperoni to cheese
  5. Bake for 12 minutes
