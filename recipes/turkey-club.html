Turkey Club

Description

This is a simple turkey club sandwich that you can easily make at home.

Save money and enjoy deliciousness at home!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Hummus
  • Siracha sauce

Steps

  1. Take out bread
  2. Cut tomato and lettuce
  3. Fry bacon
  4. Take out turkey cold cut
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add hummus and siracha to bread
  7. Assemble this beut
