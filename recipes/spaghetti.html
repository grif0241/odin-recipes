Spaghetti

Description

This is a simple spaghetti recipe that anyone can make and will only include common household ingredients.

It's also a healthy option as it uses ground turkey instead of beef for the sauce!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Garlic cloves
  • Peppers
  • Zucchini
  • Baulderson cheese
  • Ground turkey

Steps

  1. Boil 4L of water
  2. When water boiling, add 500g of noodles and cook for 10 minutes
  3. As the noodles are cooking, cut your peppers, zucchini, and garlic
  4. In a separate pan, cook the ground turkey with a small amount of oil
  5. Add diced onions and garlic to the pan and cook for 3 mintues
  6. Add remaining veggies to the pan and cooke for 4 minutes
  7. Strain noodles
  8. Enjoy!
